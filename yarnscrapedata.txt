<div class='notes markdown'>
<p>Alpaca Sox, CEYs signature sock yarn, is spun from a blend of fibers designed to make a comfortable, warm sock. Alpaca provides warmth and softness, wool adds elasticity and cushion, and a little nylon lends strength and durability.</p>

<p>The construction of Alpaca Sox is unique too. Most sock yarns are plied and tightly twisted to make them dense, smooth, and long-lasting. Alpaca Sox is a 2-ply yarn twisted just enough to be durable, but relaxed enough that the soft alpaca fibers can expand a little and show their halo.</p>

<p>The hand-dyes make effortless colorful socks when you want the yarn to do the work</p>

<p>60% Alpaca, 20% Merino Wool, 20% Nylon</p>

<p>Handwash cold, dry flat</p>

<p>Color card link at right is for hand-painted colors only.</p>
